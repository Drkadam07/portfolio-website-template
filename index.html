About Me

Hey there! I'm Dhanraj, a Mumbai-based full-stack developer who believes that great code is like great poetry - it should be elegant, purposeful, and leave a lasting impression.

My journey started at 14 when I built my first website to share my grandmother's recipes. That simple HTML page sparked a fascination with how technology could preserve and share human stories. Today, I bring that same emotional intelligence to every project, whether it's a fintech dashboard or an AI-powered recommendation engine.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me experimenting with analog photography, perfecting my grandmother's biryani recipe, or mentoring aspiring developers at local tech meetups. I believe that the best solutions come from understanding both the technical constraints and the human context.

My approach? I start every project by asking "How can this make someone's day just a little bit better?" Whether it's reducing a 5-minute task to 30 seconds or adding a delightful animation that makes users smile, I believe technology should feel magical yet intuitive.
